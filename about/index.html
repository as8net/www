About AS202361

What is AS202361?

AS202361 is a personal Autonomous System Number operated by Ask Selmer.

The network is used to test new technologies, automation tooling, and network configurations in a production environment.

As a RIPE NCC member and Local Internet Registry (LIR), AS202361 maintains its own IPv4 and IPv6 address space and participates in global internet routing through peering relationships.

LIR Services

As a RIPE NCC Local Internet Registry, we can offer the following services to other organizations:

PI Space Sponsorship

Provider Independent (PI) IPv4 and IPv6 address space sponsorship for organizations that need their own portable address space without becoming a LIR themselves.

ASN Sponsorship

Autonomous System Number sponsorship for organizations that want to run their own AS for BGP routing without the overhead of LIR membership.

RIPE Database Management

Assistance with RIPE database object creation and maintenance, including route objects, inetnum objects, and other registry data.

Interested in LIR services? Contact us to discuss your requirements and how we can help your organization get the resources you need.
